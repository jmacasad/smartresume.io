Job Title: And Highly Motivated Ict Business Analyst

Telelink Distributor is seeking an experienced and highly motivated ICT Business Analyst to join our dynamic team in our Melbourne office. This is an exciting opportunity for a strategic thinker with a deep understanding of business processes and IT systems to drive digital transformation and operational efficiency within a fast-growing organisation.

As a Business Analyst, you will play a pivotal role in identifying business needs, analysing current systems, and recommending innovative solutions. You will collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure our IT initiatives are aligned with strategic goals and deliver measurable business value.

Key Responsibilities:
Liaise with stakeholders to gather, document, and analyse business requirements.
Map current business processes using tools such as flowcharts, BPMN, or swimlane diagrams.
Analyse process performance data to identify bottlenecks and inefficiencies.
Perform gap analysis to evaluate current system capabilities against future business needs.
Design and document functional and technical specifications for IT solutions.
Collaborate with development, operations, and service teams to ensure alignment on business needs.
Lead and manage the full project lifecycle—from requirements gathering through to delivery and post-implementation review.
Facilitate Agile ceremonies including stand-ups, sprint planning, retrospectives, and showcases.
Develop and manage project plans, ensuring timely delivery within scope and budget.
Work with senior management and stakeholders to define project objectives and success criteria.
Create detailed test plans and coordinate testing to ensure high-quality system functionality.
Prepare user manuals and training materials, and support change management and system adoption.
Serve as the subject matter expert for assigned projects, supporting key decision-making processes.
Provide mentoring, support, and knowledge sharing across project teams and stakeholders.
Skills & Qualifications:
Minimum 8+ years of experience as a Business Analyst within the ICT industry.
Bachelor’s degree or higher in Business, Information Technology, or a related discipline.
Strong understanding of business analysis frameworks, documentation standards, and modelling tools.
Proficiency in business process modelling, workflow analysis, and system design.
Hands-on experience with Agile methodologies and project management tools (e.g., JIRA, Confluence, Trello).
Familiarity with business intelligence and data analysis platforms and tools.
Excellent written and verbal communication skills, with a strong ability to engage with stakeholders at all levels.
Demonstrated experience managing cross-functional project teams, including remote and offshore resources.
