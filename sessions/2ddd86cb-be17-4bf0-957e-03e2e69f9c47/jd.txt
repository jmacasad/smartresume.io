Job Title: Position Overview

Position Overview
The Regional OCM (Organizational Change Management) Lead plays a critical role in McCain Foods' digital and business transformation initiatives. This role is responsible for supporting the people side of change â€” including changes to business processes, systems, and technology. The primary focus will be creating and implementing change management strategies and plans that maximize employee adoption and minimize resistance.

This position will work to drive faster adoption, higher utilization, and greater proficiency with changes impacting employees. The OCM Regional Lead will partner closely with the Global OCM team, Regional Transformation Leads, Project Managers, Communication, HR, and Learning teams to ensure the successful adoption of initiatives.

Key Responsibilities
Develop and execute change management strategies and plans that address the people side of change, including communications, training, leadership engagement, and resistance management.

Support the design and delivery of communications and engagement plans.

Identify, analyze, and prepare risk mitigation strategies related to change impacts.

Conduct change impact assessments, stakeholder analyses, and readiness assessments.

Create actionable deliverables for major change management levers such as communication plans, training plans, sponsor roadmaps, coaching plans, and resistance management plans.

Work closely with the global and regional business teams to ensure effective stakeholder engagement.

Provide input, document requirements, and support the design and delivery of training programs.

Monitor and measure the success of change initiatives through key performance indicators (KPIs) and adjust strategies accordingly.

Ensure alignment with McCain's Global Change Management methodology.

Partner with the Regional Transformation Office and Communications teams to support integrated change strategies across regions.

Foster a culture of continuous improvement by gathering feedback post-implementation and refining approaches.

Skills and Experience Required
Bachelor's Degree in Business, Communications, Human Resources, Organizational Development, or related field.

5+ years of proven experience in organizational change management, including large-scale transformations.

Certification or training in Prosci, CCMP, or equivalent change management methodologies preferred.

Experience supporting SAP transformations (preferably SAP S/4HANA) highly desirable.

Demonstrated success in stakeholder engagement across different levels and regions.

Strong project management skills and the ability to juggle multiple priorities.

Excellent written and verbal communication skills, with the ability to create clear and engaging communications.

Analytical and problem-solving skills to identify change risks and develop appropriate strategies.

Ability to work collaboratively with global teams across different time zones.

Preferred Qualifications
Experience in a food manufacturing or consumer goods environment is an asset.

Previous experience working within a Global Transformation Program structure.

Expertise in using change management tools and Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, SharePoint).

High emotional intelligence (EQ) and relationship-building skills.
